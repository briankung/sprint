Join us for the Project Euler 5k!

Programmers and problem solvers will compete in teams of four to solve problems from Project Euler, using their own choice of programming language. Beginners and experienced programmers alike will find problems at their level, and peers to team up with. Prizes will be awarded in three categories: Beginner, Intermediate, and Open, according to the rules.

Sign up on the Meetup page for free admission!
